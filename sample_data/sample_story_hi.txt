Recipe: Dal Tadka (as I remember it)

Dal — red lentils, half pressure cooker.
Ghee — generous. Never "healthy" amounts. Generous amounts.
Cumin — whole seeds, roast until they crack. Listen for the sound.
Asafoetida — just a pinch. Eleanor insists on this.
Tomatoes — two, chopped.
Salt, turmeric, coriander powder.
At the end — fresh coriander and a squeeze of lemon.

Do not rush the cumin. Do not rush the dal. 
Cooking is patience made edible.
